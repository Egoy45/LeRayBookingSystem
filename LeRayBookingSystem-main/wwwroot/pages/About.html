About Le'Ray Aesthetic & Wellness Center

Le'Ray Aesthetic & Wellness Center is dedicated to helping our clients feel confident and beautiful in their own skin. We offer a wide range of facial, slimming, and laser treatments performed by experienced professionals in a relaxing and elegant environment.

Our mission is to provide high-quality aesthetic and wellness services that enhance your natural beauty while ensuring comfort and satisfaction.

At Le’Ray, we believe that self-care is not a luxury — it’s a necessity. Visit us and experience rejuvenation that goes beyond the surface.

FAQs

We're here to help. Search our FAQs for answers to anything you might be wondering.

How can I book an Appointment?

First, if you have no account then create one, then view services, click "Book Now," select your services, choose a date and time, and confirm your booking. Finally, proceed to payment/down payment to complete the process.

What will happen if I'm late for my Appointment?

If you're late, please contact us immediately. Late arrivals may shorten your treatment time to avoid delays for the next client.

Is there a reschedule fee?

Rescheduling within 24 hours before your appointment may incur a minimal fee. Contact our staff for assistance.

Do the store still accept walk-in appointments?

Yes, walk-ins are welcome depending on availability. We recommend booking ahead to secure your preferred time slot.
