About Le'Ray Aesthetic & Wellness Center

At Le'Ray Aesthetic & Wellness Center, we help clients feel confident and radiant in their natural beauty. Our wide range of facial, slimming, and laser treatments are performed by skilled professionals in a serene, elegant environment.

Our mission is to deliver personalized, high-quality aesthetic care that enhances your natural glow while ensuring comfort and satisfaction.

Because at Le’Ray, self-care isn’t a luxury — it’s a vital step toward confidence, balance, and wellness. Visit us and experience rejuvenation that truly goes beyond the surface.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We’re here to help. Explore our FAQs below for quick answers.

How can I book an appointment?

Simply create an account, explore our services, and click “Book Now.” Choose your preferred treatment, date, and time — then confirm with your payment.

What if I’m late to my appointment?

Please contact us as soon as possible. Late arrivals may shorten your session to avoid overlapping with the next client’s appointment.

Is there a rescheduling fee?

Rescheduling less than 24 hours before your appointment may include a small fee. Contact our staff to assist you with any changes.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, walk-ins are accepted based on availability. However, we recommend booking in advance to secure your desired schedule.
